Child Safety Standards – Dare

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Dare is committed to preventing and addressing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Any content that endangers children

Violations result in immediate removal and permanent account suspension.

Reporting System

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app. All reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly.

Moderation

We actively monitor user-generated content and take appropriate action, including:

  • Content removal
  • Account suspension or banning
  • Escalation to authorities when required

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable child protection laws and regulations. Where legally required, we report illegal content to relevant law enforcement authorities.

Age Restrictions

The app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We take reasonable steps to prevent underage access.
