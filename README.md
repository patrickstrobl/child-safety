Dare is committed to preventing and addressing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy
We strictly prohibit:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Sexual content involving minors
- Grooming or solicitation of minors
- Any content that endangers children

Violations result in immediate removal and permanent account suspension.

2. Reporting System
Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app.

All reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly.

3. Moderation
We actively monitor user-generated content and take appropriate action, including:
- Content removal
- Account suspension or banning
- Escalation to authorities when required

4. Legal Compliance
We comply with all applicable child protection laws and regulations.

Where legally required, we report illegal content to relevant law enforcement authorities.

5. Age Restrictions
The app is not intended for users under the age of 13.

We take reasonable steps to prevent underage access.
